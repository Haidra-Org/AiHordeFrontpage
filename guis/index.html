AI Horde GUIs and Tools

Explore our collection of user interfaces and developer tools for AI Horde. From web-based clients to desktop applications, mobile apps, SDKs, plugins, and bots - find everything you need to work with AI Horde.

New to AI Horde? Here's what you'll find:

AI Horde is a community-powered network where volunteers share their computers to generate AI images and text for free.

GUIs & Frontends

Easy-to-use applications for creating AI images and text. This includes web pages and mobile apps. Perfect if you just want to start making content.

Workers

Software that lets you contribute your computer's power to help others. Earn kudos (priority points) by helping the community.

Developer Tools

SDKs, libraries, and plugins for developers who want to integrate AI Horde into their own projects.

Most users start with a GUI/Frontend. We recommend ArtBot for beginners - it's web-based, so there's nothing to install. Use the filters below to find the perfect tool for your needs, or browse all available options.
